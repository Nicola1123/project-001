Our Story

A new standard in
Riyadh dining

Al-Nil Restaurant was born from a singular vision: to create a space where contemporary cuisine meets uncompromising hospitality on Riyadh's iconic Tahlia Street.

Our chefs draw inspiration from both local heritage and global technique, crafting dishes that honor seasonal ingredients while pushing creative boundaries. Every plate tells a story — we invite you to become part of it.
