Our Story

Established 2018

🏛️

Heritage

The Tahlia Cafe was born from a passion to bring authentic Saudi coffee traditions to Riyadh. We source our beans from the finest plantations in the southwestern regions of Saudi Arabia, ensuring every cup honors centuries-old brewing techniques passed down through generations.

👨‍🍳

Culinary Excellence

Our kitchen blends traditional Saudi flavors with international culinary arts. Executive Chef Mohammed Al-Rashid brings 15 years of experience from Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, creating a unique fusion that celebrates both local heritage and global innovation.

🎨

Ambiance

The interior design at The Tahlia Cafe reflects the elegant fusion of contemporary minimalism with traditional Najdi architectural elements. Handcrafted wooden panels, brass fixtures, and warm ambient lighting create an atmosphere that is both sophisticated and deeply inviting.
